# MarTech → Quant: Measurement Rigor at Execution Speed

A content series outline — bridging performance marketing attribution with trading system design.

## Thesis

Teams that mastered **attribution latency**, **incrementality**, and **idempotent event pipelines** in AdTech already understand half of systematic trading: **delayed labels**, **leakage control**, and **capital allocation under uncertainty**.
